About this site

This page includes my resume, as well as my email, LinkedIn, and GitHub links. In addition, the three figures in Problem2 are：

• Box plot of hygiene ratings for restaurants with the ten most popular cuisines in Manhattan

• In the five years from 2013 to 2017, a line chart showing the proportion of all restaurants in different cuisines receiving A grades in each year

• Histogram of the cumulative number of A-level restaurants contained in different zipcode regions

The source code can be found from: [https://github.com/chenshuopan6/chenshuopan6.github.io]
